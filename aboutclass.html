    About stem

    Senior High School (STEM)

    STEM stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It is an interdisciplinary approach to education that combines these four fields to create a holistic learning experience. STEM education encourages students to engage in hands-on activities, problem-solving, critical thinking, and collaboration to address real-world challenges.
